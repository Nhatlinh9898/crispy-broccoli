## 🎯 Tổng quan

Sử dụng các sản phẩm mẫu từ 11 loại (linh kiện công nghiệp + 6 ngành hàng + 5 loại sản phẩm) để xây dựng chương trình đào tạo toàn diện cho AI agents và content creators.

## 🎯 Assessment Framework

### **1. Skill Assessment Rubrics**

#### **Technical Writing Assessment**
```
Criteria (Weight) | Excellent (90-100%) | Good (80-89%) | Satisfactory (70-79%) | Needs Improvement (<70%)
------------------|---------------------|---------------|----------------------|------------------------
Technical Accuracy (30%) | No technical errors, all specs verified | Minor errors, specs mostly accurate | Several errors, some specs missing | Major errors, specs incorrect
Content Structure (25%) | Logical flow, clear sections, excellent organization | Good structure, minor organization issues | Adequate structure, some organization problems | Poor structure, disorganized
User Friendliness (20%) | Very easy to understand, excellent clarity | Easy to understand, good clarity | Somewhat understandable, moderate clarity | Difficult to understand, poor clarity
Completeness (15%) | All required content present, comprehensive | Most content present, minor gaps | Some content present, significant gaps | Major content missing
Compliance (10%) | Full compliance with all standards | Good compliance, minor issues | Basic compliance, some issues | Poor compliance, major issues
```

#### **Template Usage Assessment**
```
Criteria (Weight) | Excellent (90-100%) | Good (80-89%) | Satisfactory (70-79%) | Needs Improvement (<70%)
------------------|---------------------|---------------|----------------------|------------------------
Template Selection (20%) | Perfect template match for product type | Good template fit, minor mismatches | Adequate template fit, some mismatches | Poor template fit, major mismatches
Variable Usage (30%) | All variables used correctly, optimal value extraction | Most variables used correctly, good extraction | Some variables used correctly, basic extraction | Few variables used correctly, poor extraction
Customization (25%) | Excellent customization, enhances template effectiveness | Good customization, improves template | Basic customization, minimal improvement | No customization, template used as-is
Quality Output (25%) | Outstanding content quality, exceeds expectations | Good content quality, meets expectations | Adequate content quality, below expectations | Poor content quality, fails expectations
```
